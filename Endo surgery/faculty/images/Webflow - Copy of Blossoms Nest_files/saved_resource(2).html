Who We Are

Today people are living in tightly knitted groups and modern life has left us isolated. But now, people are reclaiming the social bonds that make us more human. ATNEST is the fastest growing “Managed Home Rental Network”. Going beyond the traditional real estate thinking, this next-generation home allows people to connect, collaborate and socialize while embracing the global trend of co-living spaces.Our aim to provide Comfortable and affordable rentals for all from Students to travellers who are looking for a perfect sharing nest. We believe that people want to meet likeminded people who can add value experiences while away from home. They are not just looking for a place to sleep, but a genuine human interaction while keeping their individuality and personal space. That is why we aspire to provide the right amalgamation of personal comfort and descent of people at ATNEST.
